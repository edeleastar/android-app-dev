Android Application Development

Welcome to the Mobile Application Development Module. The objective of this course will provide a comprehensive introduction to developing modern Android Applications. It is a 10 credit module, delivered over a single semester. The course will comprise of a series of lectures + labs exploring the Android development ecosystem in increasing detail. Our objective is to provide the student with the skills required to construct efficient and reliable android applications of moderately complexity. We assume a beginner/intermediate level in Java. Each week the Table of Content on the left here will be populated with a new topic - which will contain slide + labs for the week.
